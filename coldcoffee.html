COLD COFFEE

INGREDIENTS
1 tablespoon instant coffee
1/4 cup warm water
3-4 tablespoon sugar
6-8 ice cubes
DIRECTIONS
Take instant coffee, sugar and warm water in blender.
Stir very well. To make the shake, I use full cream milk, you can also use skimmed milk or low fat milk.
Blend for a minute till the coffee solution becomes frothy and the color lightens a bit.
Add ice cubes for a thick cold coffee.
Pour 2 cups chilled milk
Blend once more till everything is mixed well and you get a nice froth on top.
Pour in glasses and serve cold coffee
